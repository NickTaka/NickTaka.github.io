Interests in the Tech World

01 Sep 2023

Interests in software development first came about when I had to utilize coding to work on my projects in Astrophysics. At the time however, I had no experience in coding nor had I taken any coding courses. Upon learning the details of each research project, all students were expected to learn how to code on their own. To add further, the languages we learned had to be the same languages that the researchers were using. Thankfully, things were easily digestible as the only two projects I had worked on required python and Fortran knowledge.

With the boom in AI in recent years I felt like related projects definitely can catch the eyes of any blossoming computer science students and software development companies. The usages of AI can be seen from the largest companies in the world down to the local mom and pop stores that may utilize the technology to make improvements in their businesses. But the most fascinating yet scary developments in AI are those that emulate the creativity of the human mind, which picks apart all previously created expressions of art and amalgamates them into AI creations posted on social networking sites. I would love to dive deeper into this field, whether it be actually learning how to code such beasts, or just gain a deeper understanding of how such applications come to be.

Hope to develop skills that allow me to pursue a career in either data science or AI. Piecing these previous ideas together, I hope to pursue a career either in data science or AI related material. I still do have an interest in space related topics, and knowing how to solve problems tangential to data science can maybe help me get my foot back into the door of NASA or other space related programs. The uses of AI programs have also been proven effective in the scientific community, as solving some of the hardest or most taxing problems have become trivial with inputs from modern day programming. In some way or another, I wish to use the knowledge I gain from these computer science classes to further my understanding of the universe.
