Campus Jampus was created with 2 fellow computer science students during the summer of 2023. 
It incorperates the UH system and brings togther musically inclined people to create a site which makes it easy to connect with those that would like to play music together. 

<hr>

<pre>

  In this project I worked together with fellow CS students to create a system that would 
  register students or faculty of the UH campuses allow them ways to share not only what instruments 
  they played but also a chat and invite service that would enable them to connect should they 
  wish to engage in "Jam sessions", or inpromptu music collaborations. 
  
  This was part of the Software Quality Assurance course at UH Manoa which greatly hammered on 
  security of deployed products. Should something be released to the public, the developers 
  must be cognizant of the personal information logged and how it could fall into unwanted hands. 
  Thus security measures must be put in place, and majority of this project was finding ways to 
  create a secure system while also creating a product. 

  
</pre>
